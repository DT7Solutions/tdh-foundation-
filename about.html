About us

About Us

Empowering Lives with the TDH Foundation

The Tenali Double Horse Foundation (TDH) is dedicated to uplifting marginalized communities by promoting education, health, women empowerment, and sustainability. Through accessible resources and strategic initiatives, we aim to create resilient, knowledgeable individuals capable of building better futures. TDH focuses on bridging the gap between opportunity and need, ensuring a positive social impact in the areas we serve.

Vision

To build a society where knowledge and a positive attitude are the driving forces behind progress in education, health, sports, women empowerment, and sustainability. We envision a world where every individual, regardless of background, has the tools and opportunities to thrive, contribute to a sustainable future, and lead a life of dignity and purpose.

Mission

Our mission is to harness the power of knowledge and a positive attitude to support and uplift individuals across multiple dimensions—education, health, sports, women empowerment, and sustainability. We aim to provide financial assistance, resources, and guidance to underprivileged communities, ensuring that they have the opportunity to grow, learn, and excel. Through our initiatives, we strive to create a generation of knowledgeable, healthy, and empowered individuals who are capable of making a meaningful impact on society and the environment.

Values

  • 1. Empowerment: We believe in empowering individuals and communities to take control of their lives and create positive change.
  • 2. Knowledge: We value knowledge as the foundation for personal growth, development, and societal progress..
  • 3. Positivity: We embrace a positive attitude as a driving force for overcoming challenges and achieving success.
  • 4. Sustainability: We strive to promote environmentally sustainable practices and contribute to a healthier planet.
  • 5. Empowerment: We believe in empowering individuals and communities to take control of their lives and create positive change.
Frequently Asked Questions

Have Any Questions?

How can I donate to support education and empowerment programs?

To donate, visit our donation page, select a cause, and choose between one-time or recurring donations to support education, healthcare, or empowerment initiatives.

What are the steps to becoming a volunteer?

You can join as a volunteer by signing up on our volunteer page, filling in your details, and selecting the areas where you would like to contribute, such as health, education, or women's empowerment.

How do recurring donations work for sustainable causes?

Our platform allows you to set up recurring monthly or annual donations to ensure continuous support for key initiatives in education, healthcare, and women’s empowerment.
