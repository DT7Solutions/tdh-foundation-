who we are

The Tenali Double Horse Foundation is committed to fostering a brighter future by empowering individuals with the knowledge they need to excel in all aspects of life. We believe that knowledge, coupled with a positive attitude, is the foundation for achieving success in education, health, sports, women empowerment, and sustainability. Our foundation supports underprivileged communities by providing the resources and opportunities necessary to cultivate informed, healthy, and resilient individuals who are ready to face the challenges of tomorrow.
