IPOD Camp

IPOD Camp: for Leprosy Affected Persons

15

May

The IPOD Camp is a vital CSR initiative by the Tenali Double Horse Foundation, aimed at providing integrated prevention of deformities for leprosy-affected individuals. Partnering with LEPRA, this event focuses on delivering essential medical care and rehabilitation services.

Our mission is to improve the quality of life for leprosy-affected persons through awareness, medical intervention, and community support. We aim to eliminate the stigma associated with leprosy and empower affected individuals to reintegrate into society.

The camp will include health check-ups, physiotherapy sessions, and educational workshops, emphasizing the importance of early diagnosis and treatment. Together, we can build a supportive community for those affected by leprosy.

Join us in this noble cause to support leprosy-affected individuals. Your participation can help change lives, offering them hope and dignity.

Together, we can raise awareness and promote health education that leads to better outcomes for those impacted by leprosy. Become a part of the IPOD Camp initiative today!

For more information about the IPOD Camp and how you can get involved, visit our website or contact us. Let's make a difference in the lives of those affected by leprosy!
